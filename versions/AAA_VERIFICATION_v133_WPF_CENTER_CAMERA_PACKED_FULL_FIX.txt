IsoBlockCharacterEditor v133 - WPF packed full-grid center camera fix

- Packed full-grid scenes are procedural. _pieces only contains sparse overlays/deleted-cell helpers and must not be used as the model bounds.

- After deleting/creating holes in 256^3 mode, Center Camera could center on sparse overlay/deleted helper pieces.
- That could put the camera/target visually inside the hollow/cavity.
