IsoBlockCharacterEditor v111 - AAA scene switching + packed save fix

Base:
- v110_AAA_VoxelWorld_chunkkey_build_fix.

Fixes in v111:
1. Added ChunkedVoxelRenderer.ResetForNewScene(markDirty).
   - Drops pending async build results.
   - Advances renderer generation so old background builds cannot upload into the next scene.
   - Disposes live GPU chunks and clears renderer stats at scene boundaries.

2. Hardened ApplyPendingBuild.
   - A build result is uploaded only if its generation matches the current requested generation.
   - Older pending results are discarded and their transient lists are cleared.

3. UseNormalPieceScene and UsePackedFullGridStressScene now act as real scene boundaries.
   - Switching 256^3 -> 250K/sample/new cannot keep old 256^3 geometry or pending builds.
   - Switching normal -> 256^3 cannot keep old normal chunks.

4. GenerateStressScene now rebuilds VoxelWorld before geometry invalidation is released.
   - First draw after 10k/50k/100k/250k switch cannot see stale world data.

5. Save/load supports packed 256^3 scenes.
   - SavedScene now includes IsPackedFullGrid, PackedFullGridSize and PackedDeletedCellKeys.
   - 256^3 full scene can be saved without serializing 16.7M objects.

6. Large stress-scene GC compaction is no longer a blocking UI-thread stall.
   - Avoids apparent hangs during test switching.
